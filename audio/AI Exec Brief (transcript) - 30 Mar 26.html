New Products & Capabilities:

First, in new products and capabilities, Salesforce has made significant strides with the release of VoiceAgentRAG, a dual-agent memory router that dramatically reduces voice retrieval latency by 316 times. Additionally, Agent-Infra has launched AIO Sandbox, an all-in-one runtime for AI agents that integrates a browser, shell, shared filesystem, and multi-channel processing capabilities. This product aims to streamline the development of AI applications by providing a comprehensive environment for testing and deployment 2. Lastly, Chroma has introduced Context-1, a 20 billion parameter agentic search model designed for multi-hop retrieval and context management, which could significantly improve how AI systems understand and generate contextually relevant information 3.
